Recommended Drink:

Blue Margarita

Instructions

Rub rim of cocktail glass with lime juice. Dip rim in coarse salt. Shake tequila, blue curacao, and lime juice with ice, strain into the salt-rimmed glass, and serve.

Ingredients
Tequila
Blue Curacao
Lime juice
Salt
1 1/2 oz
1 oz

Coarse
